About

I am an astronomy Research Fellow at the Department of Physics and Astronomy "Galileo Galilei" of the University of Padova, Italy.

I focus my research study on the detection, validation, and characterization of Extrasolar planets (aka, Exoplanets), which are defined as planetary bodies outside our Solar System.

I am a keen programmer and am always willing to learn a new programming language; I am currently doing my research in Python. I also like to do astronomic observations.
